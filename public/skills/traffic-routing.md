# Traffic Routing

Allocate budget across channels by ROI, not vibes.

## Starting mix (test phase)
- TrafficJunky (tube CPM): 24% — volume + intent
- ExoClick (native/display): 20% — cheap scale
- JuicyAds (native/banner): 14% — niche targeting
- Reddit organic: 12% — compounding, free
- X/social organic: 11% — preview-safe funnel
- Creator swaps: 11% — audience-aligned, flat cost
- SEO/clips: 8% — long-tail discovery

## Optimization cadence
- Day 4: cut the bottom paid source, move budget to the top one
- Week 2: top 2 paid sources should hold 60%+ of paid spend
- Never cut organic lines — they compound while paid burns

## Rule
Route by cost-per-paid-sub, not CPC. Cheap clicks that don't convert are expensive.
