# Re-sub Winback

Recover churned subs with one well-timed message.

## Timing beats copy
Send day 3–5 after expiry — card declines resolve, attention resets.
Trigger off expiry date, not billing-failure events.

## The message
1. No guilt. No "we miss you."
2. Lead with what they missed — one concrete highlight from the week
3. One-tap re-sub link + a small returning-sub bonus

## Template shape
"You missed [specific set/moment]. It's pinned for you if you come back this week — plus [small bonus] for returning subs. [link]"

## Expectations
8–15% of churned subs recover monthly on this cadence. One message only — a second winback reads as spam and poisons the list.
