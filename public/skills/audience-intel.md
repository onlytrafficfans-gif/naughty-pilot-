# Audience Intel

Map demographics, interests, and lookalikes for adult-traffic campaigns.

## Core segments (21+ always)
- High-intent: visitors of adjacent creator pages, tube-site keyword traffic
- Interest: niche-matched communities (fitness, cosplay, lifestyle)
- Lookalike: built from existing sub list (best ROAS, needs 500+ seed)
- Retarget: page visitors who didn't convert (cheapest conversions)

## Geo tiers
- Tier 1: US, UK, CA, AU — highest LTV, highest CPM
- Tier 2: DE, FR, NL, Nordics — strong LTV, moderate CPM
- Start Tier 1 only; expand after CVR data stabilizes.

## Practical rules
- Age-gate every placement at 21+ even where 18+ is allowed — better card-approval rates
- Exclude existing subs from acquisition campaigns
- Refresh lookalikes monthly; decay is real
