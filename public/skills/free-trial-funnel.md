# Free Trial Funnel

Trial-to-paid conversion blueprint.

## Engineer the first 24 hours
1. Instant welcome DM with your single best set pinned
2. Day 2: interactive touch — poll or question — to build the open habit
3. Day 5: preview of sub-only content they can't open yet
4. Expiry day: convert with a first-month offer, not a plea

## Trial length
7 days. 30-day trials kill urgency and train freeloading.

## Conversion message shape
"Your trial wraps today — stay on and [this week's drop] unlocks the second you do. First month [offer]."

## Targets
- Trial→paid: 20–30%
- If below 15%: the wall content isn't differentiated enough from the trial — fix content, not copy
