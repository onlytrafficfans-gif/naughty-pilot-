# Launch Sequencer

Step-by-step campaign go-live checklist.

## T-72h
- [ ] Creative approved against the strictest network policy
- [ ] Landing link tested on mobile — every hop, logged out
- [ ] Tracking pixel + postback verified with a test conversion

## T-24h
- [ ] Budget caps set per channel (daily, not lifetime)
- [ ] Audience exclusions applied (existing subs, staff IPs)
- [ ] First-48h creative rotation loaded (3 variants minimum)

## T-0 launch
- [ ] Launch at the audience's peak-traffic window, not yours
- [ ] Verify spend is pacing within 2h — a stuck campaign wastes the window

## T+48h
- [ ] Kill bottom variants by CTR
- [ ] Check cost-per-sub against forecast floor
- [ ] Reallocate to top source; document what moved
