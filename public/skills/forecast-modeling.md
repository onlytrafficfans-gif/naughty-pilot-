# Forecast Modeling

Project subs and revenue from spend inputs — directionally, honestly.

## The chain
Spend → clicks (CPC) → landing visits (CTR) → subs (CVR) → revenue (price × subs)

## Baseline rates (adult traffic, test phase)
- CPC: $0.08–0.15 on tube CPM inventory
- Click→visit: 35–55% (link friction kills the rest)
- Visit→paid: 2–5% (creative quality is the swing factor)

## How to model
1. Use the pessimistic end of every range for the floor
2. Use midpoints for the plan number
3. Never promise the optimistic end — treat it as upside

## After week 1
Replace every assumed rate with your measured rate. A forecast that ignores its own data is a story, not a model.
