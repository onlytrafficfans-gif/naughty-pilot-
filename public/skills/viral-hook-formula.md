# Viral Hook Formula

Turn any photo into a scroll-stopping first line.

## Structure
[Pattern break] + [Curiosity gap] + [Implied payoff]

1. Open with a statement that contradicts expectation ("I almost deleted this one.")
2. Leave one specific detail unsaid — curiosity drives the click, not description
3. Never explain the photo; tease the story behind it

## Examples of the shape (adapt to your voice)
- "I wasn't going to post this."
- "The one my photographer said wouldn't work."
- "Everyone asks about this set. Nobody guesses right."

## Testing
Write 3 hooks per creative. Run 48h. Keep the top CTR, archive the rest with notes.
