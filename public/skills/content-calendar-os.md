# Content Calendar OS

30-day posting cadence for max algorithmic reach.

## Weekly spine
- 3 feed posts: Mon/Wed/Fri at your audience's peak hours (check per-platform analytics)
- Daily: stories/casual touchpoints
- 1 collab or shoutout swap
- 1 wall-exclusive drop, announced 24h ahead on socials

## Batching
One 3-hour shoot per month = 12+ feed assets + 20+ story frames.
Shoot in 3 outfit/set changes; every change is a content week.

## Weekly review (15 min)
- Rank the week's posts by CTR to page link
- Double down on the top format next week
- Cut the bottom format entirely

## Reality check
The calendar compounds — reach builds week 3+, not day 3. Consistency beats bursts; a missed week costs more than a mediocre post.
