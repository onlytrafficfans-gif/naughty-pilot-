# Conversion Copy

Write captions, bios, and CTAs that convert for adult-creator campaigns.

## Bio formula
1. Who you are in 5 words (identity, not adjectives)
2. What subs actually get (concrete: cadence + content type)
3. One CTA with a reason to act now

## Caption structure
- Line 1: hook (pattern break or curiosity gap — never describe the photo)
- Line 2: context or story fragment
- Line 3: CTA framed as an invitation, not a plea

## CTA rules
- One CTA per asset. Two CTAs = zero CTAs.
- Verbs of access ("unlock", "join", "get in") beat verbs of commerce ("buy", "purchase").
- Never mention the platform's name in paid-ad copy (policy risk).

## Testing
Run 3 caption variants per creative for 48h. Kill the bottom two. CTR to page-link is the only vote that counts.
