# DM Sequences

Automate warm-up to conversion messages without sounding automated.

## The 3-touch flow
1. **T+5min after follow**: personal thank-you + one specific detail. No pitch.
2. **T+24h**: free value — a preview, a behind-the-scenes note. Still no pitch.
3. **T+48h**: the invitation. One link, one reason, one deadline-free ask.

## Voice rules
- Write like one person texting one person
- No emoji walls, no ALL CAPS, no "hey babe" openers to cold traffic
- Match the reply's energy: short reply → short response

## Segmented follow-ups
- Engaged but not subbed (7 days): send the strongest single preview
- Subbed, low engagement (14 days): interactive touch — question or poll
- Expired (day 3–5): winback with a concrete highlight they missed
