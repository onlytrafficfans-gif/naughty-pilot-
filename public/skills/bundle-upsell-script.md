# Bundle Upsell Script

Turn one purchase into three.

## The window
The 10 minutes after a purchase is your highest-intent moment. Automate the send.

## The script shape
"Since you grabbed [X], the [X+Y+Z] bundle is yours for [single price +60%] — only offered right after a purchase."

## Rules
- Bundle must be thematically linked to the trigger purchase
- Frame the discount as *access* ("only offered right after"), not a markdown
- One follow-up maximum, 24h later, then never again for that purchase

## Building the matrix
Map every purchasable item → its two best companions. Pre-write the bundle message for each. Automation without the matrix is spam.

## Target
25–35% attach rate. Below 15% = the bundle isn't actually related.
