# Scarcity Trigger

Limited-time language that converts without lying.

## Only use real limits
Fake countdowns burn trust and kill re-bills. Real scarcity only:

1. **Capacity**: "First 50 subs get X" — and count it down truthfully
2. **Time-boxed bonus**: the bonus expires, the price doesn't change
3. **Content retirement**: "This set leaves the wall Friday" — and it does

## Announcement cadence
- Announce the deadline once early (48h out)
- Remind once, ~3h before
- Never extend a stated deadline. Ever. The next deadline depends on it.

## Anti-patterns
- Permanent "sales" (trains everyone to never pay full price)
- Countdown timers that reset
- "Almost sold out" on unlimited digital goods
