# Ad Variation Engine

Generate 9 ad variants from one creative — systematically, not randomly.

## The 3×3 matrix
Axes: Hook type × CTA frame
- Hooks: curiosity gap / bold claim / story fragment
- CTAs: access ("unlock it") / scarcity ("this week only") / identity ("for the ones who get it")

Every cell = one variant. Same image, nine distinct messages.

## Crop variants
- 1:1 for native placements
- 9:16 for mobile interstitial
- 16:9 for banner — face stays in-frame in every crop

## Testing protocol
1. Launch all 9 at equal budget for 48h
2. Kill bottom 6 by CTR
3. Scale top 3; refresh creative when frequency > 3
