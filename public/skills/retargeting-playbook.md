# Retargeting Playbook

Re-engage visitors who didn't convert — the cheapest subs you'll ever buy.

## Segments & messages
- Visited, bounced <10s: different hook entirely — the first one failed
- Visited, browsed, no click: social proof angle ("the set everyone joined for")
- Clicked join, didn't pay: friction message — reassure on privacy + billing discretion
- Expired subs: winback at day 3–5 (see Re-sub Winback)

## Rules
- Frequency cap 3/day — retargeting fatigue reads as desperation
- Exclude converters within 1h of conversion
- Retargeting budget: 15–20% of total; it converts 3–5× acquisition CVR

## Creative
Never re-show the exact ad they ignored. Same model, different angle, different promise.
